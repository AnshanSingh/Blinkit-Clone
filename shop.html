Frequently Asked question

1. What is the best rolling paper for beginners?

Pre-rolled cones are the easiest option, as they eliminate the need for manual rolling.

2. Are unbleached rolling papers better?

Yes, unbleached papers are free from chemicals and provide a cleaner, more natural smoke.

3. Do you offer flavoured rolling papers?

Yes, we have a variety of flavoured papers, including fruit, chocolate, and mint.

4. How can I store rolling papers to keep them fresh?

Keep them in a dry place, away from moisture and direct sunlight, to prevent warping or tearing.

5. Which brands offer the thinnest rolling papers?

Brands like Stash Pro, Bongchie, and Mozo offer ultra-thin rolling papers for a slow and even burn.

6. Do rolling papers affect the taste of the smoke?

Yes, the material and thickness of the paper can influence the taste. Hemp and rice papers tend to provide a cleaner taste, while flavoured papers add an extra element to your smoking experience.

7. What is the difference between rice, hemp, and wood pulp rolling papers?

Rice papers are ultra-thin and burn slowly, giving a smooth smoking experience.

Hemp papers are slightly thicker, burn evenly, and have a mild natural flavour.

Wood pulp papers are more traditional, slightly thicker, and easier to roll with.
